# AI Image Generation Guide: Flowers Champ Luxury

This guide contains heavily researched, structured prompts to help you generate beautiful, brand-compliant banner images using Freepik's AI Image Generator (or Midjourney). 

For the highest-end look, it is highly recommended to use the **Flux** or **Mystic** style models in Freepik, as they handle complex textures, lighting, and photorealism much better than standard models.

---

## 📸 Core Prompt Formula for Freepik
Freepik AI responds best when you think like a creative director. Use this structure for your prompts:
`[Hero Product] + [Setting/Surface] + [Lighting] + [Mood/Style] + [Technical Details] + [Negative Prompts]`

> [!TIP]
> **No Text Padding Needed!** 
> Your website handles the split-screen design via code. You do **not** need to generate images with empty space on the left. Tell the AI to keep the floral arrangement beautifully centered.

---

## 🎨 Scenarios & Prompts

### 1. The Standard Luxury Banner
Use this when you need a general, evergreen banner for the website.

```text
A luxury floral bouquet featuring fresh, high-end blooms like lush roses, peonies, and orchids in soft romantic pastels and rich ruby reds. Placed on a dark charcoal marble surface. Cinematic studio lighting, soft diffused shadows, subtle warm gold accents in the environment. Dark, moody, and elegant atmosphere complementing deep olive-green tones. Professional editorial product photography, sharp focus on the flowers, shallow depth of field, 8k resolution, highly detailed, photorealistic.
```

### 2. Converting an Existing Image (Image-to-Image)
If you have a photo of a bouquet (e.g., taken in your shop) and you want Freepik AI to "restyle" it to match your website's dark-olive and gold aesthetic, use the **Image-to-Image** (or 'Reimagine') feature and provide this prompt:

```text
Retain the exact structure and floral arrangement of the original image, but elevate it to a high-end luxury editorial style. Apply a moody, sophisticated lighting setup with soft shadows. Change the background to a deep olive-green or dark charcoal aesthetic. Add subtle, warm gold highlights to the lighting. Professional 50mm lens photography, 8k resolution, photorealistic, premium floral boutique aesthetic.
```
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Set the "Image Influence" or "Image Weight" slider to around 60-70%. If it's too high, it won't change the lighting; if it's too low, the bouquet will look completely different from your original photo.

### 3. Valentine's Day / Romantic Occasion
```text
A stunning, opulent bouquet of deep crimson red roses and dark burgundy dahlias. Placed elegantly on a dark velvet surface. Soft, romantic candle-lit studio lighting with rich, moody shadows. A subtle gold ribbon tied around the stems. Luxurious, passionate, and elegant atmosphere. Professional commercial photography, macro details, 8k resolution, masterpiece.
```

### 4. Weddings & Bridal
```text
An elegant, pure white and soft blush bridal bouquet featuring white orchids, hydrangeas, and soft eucalyptus leaves. Set against a dark, moody charcoal and olive-green background for dramatic contrast. Soft, ethereal window lighting, elegant Vogue-style photography. Clean, minimalist luxury, sharp focus, f/2.8 aperture, 8k resolution.
```

### 5. Luxury Gift Combos (Flowers + Chocolates)

Cinematic rim lighting, emphasizing the luxurious textures of the petals and chocolate. Moody, high-end product photography, deep olive background tones, photorealistic, highly detailed.

## 🚫 Negative Prompts
If Freepik gives you a box for "Negative Prompts" (things you *don't* want the AI to generate), always include this string to prevent errors:

```text
text, watermark, logo, typography, blurry, distorted, low quality, messy background, bright white background, cheap, plastic, fake flowers, overexposed
```
